ABOUT

The Ambassadors Of Comedy is a live stand-up show featuring some of America's funniest foreign comedians. This hilarious theme show consists of routines about life in the United States from a foreigner's perspective. The Ambassadors Of Comedy motto is: "If you can't go on vacation, we'll bring the vacation to you."
